What is a fantasy book?

Definition â–¾

Fantasy is fiction built around elements that do not exist in our world: magic, mythical creatures, invented worlds, and supernatural rules. The focus is not on realism, but on consistent world logic.

In this project, fantasy books are organized by tags such as epic, dark, heist, dragons, elements, and curses.
